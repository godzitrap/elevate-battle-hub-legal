Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 24, 2026

1. Overview

This Privacy Policy explains how Elevate Battle Hub collects and uses information when you use the application.

2. Age Restriction

Elevate Battle Hub is intended for individuals who are 18 years of age or older. We do not knowingly collect or store information from individuals under the age of 18.

3. Information We Collect

We collect only publicly available profile information provided during TikTok authentication, such as username, profile image, and public account identifiers.

4. Information We Do Not Collect

We do not collect passwords, private messages, email addresses, phone numbers, payment information, or any non-public TikTok data.

5. How Information Is Used

Public profile information is used solely to authenticate users and display basic profile details within the application for matchmaking purposes.

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share user information with third parties.

7. Data Retention

Public profile information may be retained only as long as necessary to support application functionality.

8. User Rights

Users may request deletion of their information by contacting:
support@elevatebattlehub.app

9. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page.
